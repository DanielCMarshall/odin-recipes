Jerk Chicken

Prep time

  • 30 Minutes Per serving

Ingredients

  • 5 tablespoons Jamaican Jerk Spice
  • tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 upvinegar (apple cider or white distilled work fin)
  • 2 pounds bone-in skin-on chicken
  • tablespoons fresh lime juice (1 small lime)

Instructions

Mix the marinade ingredients together, pour over the chicken, and refrigerate overnight (at least 4 to 6 hours, and up to 2 days). Zip lock bags make great disposable marinating containers. Throw the left-over marinade away. Grill as desired.
